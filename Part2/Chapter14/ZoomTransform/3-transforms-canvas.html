Multiple programmatic transforms with d3.zoom().transform() using Canvas

Transforms:

  1. trsf1 = d3.zoomIdentity.translate(-400,-300).scale(2)
  2. trsf2 = trsf1.translate(200,150).scale(0.25)
  3. trsf3 = d3.zoomIdentity

Interactive zoom is also enabled: double-click to zoom in, shift-double-click to zoom out, or use mouse wheel / pinch touchscreen, drag to pan.
